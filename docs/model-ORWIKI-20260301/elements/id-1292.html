Assess Organizational Changes ()
The activities associated with determining and documenting the impact of process and supply chain network changes on resources and skills. The purpose is to determine the high-level or gross requirements for hiring, re-deployment, training in support of the proposed process and network changes. This may include assessing changes required in organization structures (who reports to whom), but this is not always a requirement. It is important to agree and document how changes to performance goals will cascade through the different organizations.
