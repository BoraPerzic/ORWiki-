Perform Technology Organizational Analysis ()
The activities associated with reviewing the roles and responsibilities of technology assets. This includes budget responsibility and vendor relationship management and contract management responsibilities. The objective of Technology Organizational Analysis is to document roles and responsibilities for technology (read: software and hardware instances) for the purpose of analyzing conflicts and deficits.
