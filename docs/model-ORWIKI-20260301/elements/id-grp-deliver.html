D Deliver ()
Deliveris the macro process representing the processes associated with managing orders and shipments for products and services. Deliver processes are required for supply chains to acceptt:SOs, fulfill these orders and invoice customers. Customers generally primarily interact with a supply chain throughDeliverprocesses. TheDeliverprocess includes order management, warehousing, transportation, managing Import/Export.
