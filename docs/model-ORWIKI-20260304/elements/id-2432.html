Schedule Make Orders ()
The activities associated with validating material and production asset availability, assigning orders to production assets, sequencing production activities and monitoring and correcting production progress. This is aM2process; Customer orders are the only type of demand and distinctly identifiable in the process. Planning primarily focuses on positioning capacity and scheduling primarily focuses on schedule adherence (as a change in the schedule impacts a customer order directly).
