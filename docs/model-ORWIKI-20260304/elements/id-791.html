Make-to-Order ()
The collection of processes associated with producing sales order specific products. The purpose ofMake-to-Orderis to produce or finalize products based on actual customer orders and/or customer configuration. Make-to-Orderis a supply chain configuration of the Make process that requires the customer order to be reflected in theMakeprocess without aggregation/disaggregation. This typically means the customer order is trackable and traceable through the manufacturing process. A customer order cancellation will impact a specific production order directly. Make-to-Orderis theMakeprocess that facilitates theMTOSupply Chain Strategy.
