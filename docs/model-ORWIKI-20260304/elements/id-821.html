Deliver-From-Stock ()
The processes associated with order management and order fulfillment for stocked products. This includes order creation, scheduling, transportation scheduling, order picking, packing, shipping and invoicing.Deliver-From-Stockis the Deliver process that facilitates theMTSSupply Chain Strategy. This typically means that for planning purposes demand is aggregated and replenished as generic* stock.
