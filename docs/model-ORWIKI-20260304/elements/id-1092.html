Perform Network Organizational Analysis ()
The activities associated with reviewing the roles and responsibilities at the network node level. The objective of Network Organizational Analysis is to document roles and responsibilities for nodes and high level processes across the supply chain network for the purposes of analyzing conflicts and deficits, and to clarify for communication where various inputs/outputs are required.
