Invoice ()
The activities associated with preparing and issuing invoices and other documentation as required. This may include detailed specifications of the product or service configuration delivered as contractually required or customary. Where applicable this may include triggering the payment draw process. For example settling a credit card payment or drawing aLetter of Credit.
